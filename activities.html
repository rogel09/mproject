Basketball Tournament

Basketball Tournament is an annual event of San Nicolas, which held from the second week of November and ends with the first week of December. This is to show the talents of San Nicolenos in playing Basketball.

Christmas Lighting

Christmas Lighting is an annual event of San Nicolas, which held from the second week of december. This is to show the love and festive spirit and create a magical warm holiday season.
